# customer_shopping_behavior_pandas_sql_powerBI

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping behavior in the retail and e-commerce industry. The primary objective of this analysis is to understand customer purchasing patterns and generate actionable business insights that help improve revenue, customer retention, and marketing performance.

## Business Objective
How can a retail company understand customer purchasing behavior to:Increase overall revenue
- Improve customer retention
- Optimize marketing strategies
- Identify high-value customers
- Improve product performance
- Enhance customer experience

## Dataset Information
Numerical Features
- Age
- Purchase Amount
- Previous Purchases
- Review Rating

Categorical Features
- Category
- Product Name
- Gender
- Season
- Payment Method
- Shipping Type
- Subscription Status
- Discount Applied

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Pandas for Data Cleaning & Transformation
- SQL Server for Data Analysis & Business Query
- Power BI for Dashboard & Visualization

## Project Workflow
1. Data Collection
- Collected retail/e-commerce customer transaction dataset from Kaggle.

2. Data Cleaning & Transformation using Pandas
- The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Python Pandas in Jupyter Notebook.
  
#### Tasks Performed:
- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values
- Standardized categorical columns
- Fixed inconsistent data
- Converted data types

#### Exported cleaned dataset to SQL Server for analysis

3. SQL Server Analysis
Business questions were solved using SQL queries in SQL Server.

#### Analysis Performed:
- Revenue Analysis
- Customer Segmentation
- Discount Effectiveness Analysis
- Product Performance Analysis
- Subscription Behavior Analysis
- Gender-Based Revenue Analysis
- Shipping Type Analysis
- Age Group Revenue Analysis

4. Power BI Dashboard Development
An interactive dashboard was created in Power BI to visualize customer behavior and business performance.

#### Dashboard Features:
- KPI Cards
- Interactive Filters
- Revenue Analysis
- Customer Segmentation
- Product Performance
- Subscription Analysis
- Shipping Analysis
- Gender & Age Group Insights

### Business Problems Solved
- The company does not know which product category contributes most to overall revenue.
- Discounts may reduce profits without significantly improving sales performance.
- The company lacks visibility into revenue contribution by gender segments.
- The company cannot identify high-spending customers who are also discount users.
- There is no visibility into product performance based on customer satisfaction.
- The company does not know whether faster shipping leads to higher customer spending.
- The effectiveness of subscription programs is unclear.
- Some products may be overly dependent on discounts.
- Lack of customer segmentation leads to generic marketing strategies.
- The company does not know the best-performing products within each category.
- The relationship between customer loyalty and subscription behavior is unclear.
- The company lacks visibility into which age groups contribute most to revenue.

### Dashboard KPIs
The Power BI dashboard includes the following KPI metrics:
- Total Customers
- Unique Products
- Average Review Rating
- Total Revenue
- Average Spend
- Revenue by Shipping Type
- Revenue by Gender
- Revenue by Subscription
- Revenue by Category
- Customer Segmentation
- Top & Bottom Products

### Key Insights
- Electronics category generated the highest revenue.
- Loyal customers formed the largest customer segment.
- Subscription customers contributed significantly to overall revenue.
- Express shipping customers showed strong revenue contribution.
- Discount strategies strongly influenced purchasing behavior for certain products.
- Older age groups generated higher spending compared to younger customers.
- Customer segmentation helped identify high-value customers.

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates how retail businesses can use data analytics to better understand customer purchasing behavior and make data-driven decisions.
By combining Python, SQL Server, and Power BI, raw transactional data was transformed into actionable business insights that support:
- Revenue Growth
- Customer Retention
- Marketing Optimization
- Product Performance Improvement
- Better Customer Experience
